Biography

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, working with Prof. Yiqun Liu. I received my Ph.D. degree from Renmin University of China, supervised by Prof. Zhicheng Dou at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence.

My research mainly focuses on information retrieval and natural language processing. I am currently working on applying language models to IR problems including personalization, social simulation, retrieval-augmented generation, LLM agents, and health-related model audits.
